Getting myself involved in Transportation Engineering for almost 5 years (2017-2022), I'm here to share my feelings and experiences.\
 \
Transportation Engineering focus on 2 key words, **Safety** and **Efficiency**. In other words, it helps people travel safely and save much time in the meantime. Following this idea and combining with different types of transport, this huge field can be divided into several parts, like air traffic, maritime traffic, urban transportation and motorway traffic. Going from the other side, there are many up-to-date research topics, like transportation planning, transportation economy, transportation data science and autonomous vehicles. \
 \
In the past five years, my research focus on urban transportation, traffic data analysis and autonomous vehicles. Also, I have attended some air traffic courses, given by Prof. Mark Hansen in UC Berkeley. Transportation data science is an interesting topic and we use data analysis to improve transportation management and planning, in order to realize **Safety** and **Efficiency** ultimately. With the development of computer science and information technology, autonomous vehicles become the main stream in transportation engineering and there are many fashion research topics to be done. That's why this year, I began to do some researches in autonoumous vehicles.\
 \
Those are the feelings I'd love to share. You can found some details on my personal website. Warm welcome to send me emails to discuss with me about transportation engineering.\
